# Ecosystem & Incentives

Chance is built around aligned incentives.

## Roles

**Stakers** – secure the oracle layer and earn fees.

**Forecasters** – improve market accuracy.

**LPs** – support liquidity across market types.

**Builders** – create products, indices, and structured markets.

**Institutions** – use indices for risk and exposure.

**AI Agents** – participate in forecasting or trading.

## Incentives

- accuracy mining
- liquidity mining
- staker rewards
- index creation incentives
- governance rewards

## Treasury

Funds:

- oracle research
- public-good indices
- market subsidies
- partnerships
- audits

## Ecosystem Growth

- hackathons
- forecasting tournaments
- oracle challenges
- grants for index builders
- strategic integrations
